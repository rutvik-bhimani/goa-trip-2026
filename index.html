🥃 MUST-TRY DRINKS
🥃
Cashew Feni — The Soul of Goa
Made from fermented cashew apples. Protected GI product. 40–45% ABV. Try neat, then with lime soda. Brands: Cazulo, Big Boss, Sanjeevani. ~₹200–400/bottle.
🥥
Coconut Feni / Toddy
Distilled from coconut sap. Lighter, nuttier. Locals love it. Also ask for fresh coconut toddy (niro) if available.
🍹
Feni Cocktails
Feni Mojito (feni + lime + mint + soda) or Feni Collins. Available at most beach restaurants. Refreshing and local.
🍺
Kingfisher Beer
India's #1 beer. Cold Kingfisher + Goan fish curry = perfection. ~₹150–250 at restaurants.

🚨 NEVER swim in the sea during monsoon. Dangerous currents kill experienced swimmers every year. Red flags = no entry. Stick to pools and calm lagoons.

🏆 LOCAL PRO TIPS
☀️ Do outdoor activities before noon — rain usually comes in the afternoon
🌧️ Rain comes in bursts — wait it out at a café then go again
📵 Download Google Maps area offline before you go
🕺 Restaurants much less busy — easier to get good tables
💰 Off-season = vendors more willing to bargain at markets
🤤 Always confirm tours the morning of — some cancel in heavy rain
